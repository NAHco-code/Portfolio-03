about.

Hello! My name is Kathryn I'm a full stack web developer with an affinity for front-end design. My interest in web development started at the beginning of 2020 when the pandemic emerged. On awarded scholarship, I completed a MERN Stack bootcamp with Trilogy - now edX - in 2021. And being a millennial - having grown up along-side the birth of the internet - turned out to a be surprising advantage while honing the skill.

Fast-forward to today, I now have the privilege of guiding other hopefuls through the web development bootcamp experience. My main focus at work these days is building more efficient, user-friendly documents & systems to organize & distribute information, tools, & resources for students and staff, and performing as lead instructor.

I've also recently been coordinating the development, editing, and implementation of a reusable curriculum for the JWD and JFS Programs - and of course there are always a few side projects!
